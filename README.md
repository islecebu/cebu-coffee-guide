# cebu-coffee-guide
A personal guide to exploring Cebu’s cafés — cozy spots, specialty brews, and hidden gems for coffee lovers.
Exploring Cebu’s Café Scene ☕

During my recent trip to Cebu, I set out thinking I’d just grab a quick cup of coffee… but I ended up spending an entire day café-hopping! Cebu’s coffee culture completely surprised me.

From cozy work-friendly spots to aesthetic cafés perfect for Instagram, the city has a coffee shop for every mood. Each place I visited offered something unique — from specialty espresso drinks to affordable, budget-friendly local flavors.

Cebu isn’t just about beaches and nightlife anymore; it’s quickly becoming a coffee lover’s paradise. Many cafés now focus on high-quality beans, locally sourced ingredients, and comfortable environments with WiFi — ideal for remote workers, travelers, and coffee enthusiasts.
